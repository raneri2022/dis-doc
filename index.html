Steps of Instruction¶

Overview¶

On the landing page the applicant has an option to create an account. It must be noted that the applicant must have a valid email account in order to create his or her account. Once the applicant enters a valid email address, he or she will receive an access code on his or her email.

Then the applicant is required to copy the four digits access code and insert it in the verification input page. After completing these steps, the applicant will proceed to request registration page where the applicant is expected to fill in his or her preliminary clear and authentic information or data. Then the applicant has to submit his or her questionnaire by clicking the submit option.

Subsequently, the data reaches the administrator’s server. Accordingly, the committee responsible for verifying the applicant’s details verifies the preliminary data entered by the applicant and gives either response:

1. If the applicant’s request is approved, he or she will receive access code and a link in his/her email.

2. If the applicant’s request is not approved, the applicant will receive “sorry message” in his or her email.

Applicant’s whose requests are approved have to use the access code and the link they received on their email and will be able to access the actual questionnaire page and can start filling in their details.

Once the questionnaire is filled in correctly the applicant will receive “final success message” on the questionnaire page itself.

Deployment View¶

Deployment on local servers.

To locally deploy the project in local servers The Server must be compatible with high specification for handling large data set with higher device specifications. To operate on large data scale having a server that is able to handle different types of scenarios is a mandatory thing to acquire. Thus to have ultimate system efficiency servers with high performance are recommended in this case, from our research the minimum requirements for running the server are as follows.

1. High-Capacity Servers.

The ideal properties of a big data server are massive storage, ultra-fast recovery, and high-end analytical capability. So, you need the servers that have the right configuration and capacities to meet all these requirements without any compromise. Volume. As the name suggests, the big data feeds on loads of data that can go up to petabytes. For the uninformed, a single Petabyte is equal to 1,000,000 GB. So, make sure that your server can not only handle this massive amount of data but can also continue working consistently while handling it. Real-Time Analysis. The USP of big data is organizing and structuring a huge volume of diverse and unstructured data and seamlessly adding the latter to the available structured data. So, you would need the servers with very high processing capacities to handle this requirement efficiently without fail. Retrieval capabilities. Big data has big objectives too. For instance, real-time stock trading analysis where even a fraction of seconds matters a lot and can introduce multiple changes. For that, your server should fully support multiple users who are concurrently adding multiple inputs every second.

2. Sufficient Memory.

RAM is one of the prime requirements for big data analytics tools and applications. Using RAM instead of storage will significantly accelerate the processing speed and help you to gain more output in relatively less time. It translates to better productivity and quicker time-to-market – the two factors that offer you a competitive edge in the industry. Due to varying requirements in terms of volumes and operations, it is not possible to advice on a typical RAM volume. However, to be on the safer side it is good to go with at least 64GB RAM. The readers are advised to discuss their requirements with the providers to know about the ideal memory requirements for their purpose..

3. Processor.

The central processing unit (CPU) of a computer chip is the heart of any hosting server When choosing a processor for a working server, keep the following in mind: cost and return (but not cost) known as much as processing speed. There are also two main types of processors: Intel and AMD. Both work and you can choose the best plan for your budget plan. Usually, the performers have a high degree of stiffness and a high return rate, resulting in a complete breakdown. Remember, however, that multi-core processors may work better, but their clock speed is slightly lower than that of a single manufacturer. Each processor has more than 8 cores and a frequency over 3 GHz should be enough for most cores. The suggested processor is from Intel Xeon Scalable series depending on the budget.

4. Sufficient Network Capacity

You would need to send massive data volumes to the server. Lack of sufficient network capacity can throttle your operations. Be considerate of the fluctuations as well. You wouldn’t regularly be writing huge data volumes, which means that buying high bandwidth plans isn’t a cost-efficient solution for you. So, opt for the bespoke bandwidth solutions that allow you to select the ideal bandwidth to competently fulfill your data transfer requirements. You can choose different bandwidth packages starting from 20 TB and going up to 1000 TB per month. To make things easier you may like to inform your provider about your expected data transfer requirements and ask them about the ideal bandwidth volume. Reputed providers can also offer you unmetered bandwidth for more demanding enterprise clients. Depending upon the volume and frequency of data 1Gbps is the least amount of bandwidth you require for your server.

5. Manpower of operation.

The needed man power to operate the system relies on the users (all Eritrean diaspora) number of people. Thus to verify large number of requests at a time the central server needs more people to handle the requests at the shortest time possible, The number of people that are estimated for handling minimum are 4 persons per server with computers that are connected to the server on real time scenarios. Also the person assigned to handle this system have to have basic knowledge of the computer fields.
